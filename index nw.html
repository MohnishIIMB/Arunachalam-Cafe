Our Story

About Arunachalam Café

A journey from the sacred hills of Tamil Nadu to the heart of Raipur.

🏺

Arunachalam Café

Est. in Raipur

Rooted in Tradition, Served with Heart

Arunachalam Café was born from a deep reverence for the ancient town of Tiruvannamalai — home of the sacred Arunachaleswarar Temple — and its rich culinary traditions that have nourished pilgrims and families for centuries.

Our founders brought with them family recipes, a belief in pure vegetarian cooking, and a vision to recreate the warmth of a South Indian tiffin house in the heart of Chhattisgarh. Every dosa, every idli, every filter coffee is a tribute to that heritage.

The walls of our café are adorned with classical murals, kolam patterns, and a hand-painted temple scene — reminders that food, at its best, is a form of devotion.
